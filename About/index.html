Hi, I'm Kisara Cloutier, and I'm currently training to be a full-stack developer. After graduating with a BS in Physics, I realized how much I loved coding through my computational physics courses. I loved making interactable content and building programs from the ground up. So, after graduation, I took some time off to study this newfound interest and found myself gravitating towards web design and development!

In addition to coding, I also enjoy other hobbies such as drawing, playing video games, singing, and playing the guitar. I love any activity or project that allows me to be creative, and I hope to put that creativity into everything I do.
